- The back spasms that ailed Marshawn Lynch last season are already acting up; he’s questionable for week 1.

- If Jonathan Stewart ends up sitting DeAngelo Williams should see a very nice number of carries.

- Obviously Green Bay brought in Cedric Benson to be their lead back, but with James Starks ruled out it’s possible the Pack take this opportunity to give Alex Green a few touches to see how his reconstructed knee is doing.

- Arian Foster apparently has some kind of knee thing; the word is the Texans are just being cautious. I really hope so.

- Seems like the Steelers and Vikings (Mendenhall and Peterson respectively) are keeping their star running backs questionable to force defenses to account for them. Neither is likely to see many carries this week, but Peterson might be ready for a moderate load by week 2. The outlook for Mendenhall is probably late-September.

- Second string Patriots RB Shane Vereen may miss this week, potentially allowing Stevan Ridley to put a stranglehold on his starting spot. Still… who knows?

- Receivers Marques Colston (NO), Denarius Moore (OAK), and Stevie Johnson (BUF) are all listed as questionable. Keep an eye on them.
